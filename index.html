Mr. Electromedical is a
Medical Robot

The robot is capable of measuring body temperature, heart rate, and blood pressure. Five students of Brahmanbaria Polytechnic Institute have developed a wonderful robot. The robot can be used as an option to a physician. The robot has been named 'Mr. Electromedical'. The robot is in the process of adding alcohol detectors and fire alarms as a new feature to detect and report drug addiction.

About Mr. Electromedical

Robot can be used as
an option to a physician

Mr. Electromedical is able to perform a number of tests necessary to diagnose human body blood pressure, blood volume, ECG, heartbeat, cholesterol, uric acid and blood sugar measurements. The robot has been added to the BP monitor, ECG sensor pulse oximetry sensor, GCU sensor, fingerprint sensor and thermal scanner. Camera and ultrasonic sensors have been fitted for movement.

Biomedical Parameter Measurements
  • Body Temperature
  • Blood Pressure
  • Oxygen Saturation
  • Pulse Rate
  • Blood Glucose
  • Blood Cholesterol
  • Uric Acide

Who made Mr. Electromedical Robot

Brahmanbaria polytechnic Institute's students develop "Mr. Electromedical" robot. Five students of Brahmanbaria Polytechnic Institute have developed a wonderful robot. The robot can be used as an option to a physician. Five eighth batch studenst of the Department of Electromedical Technology of Brahmanbaria Polytechnic Institute, Ashikur Rahman, Md. Anasur Rahman, Md. Mir Amin, Mehdi Hassan and sixth batch student Abdul Monnaf manufactured the 'Mr. Electromedical' robot in just 15 days.


The robot is under the control of

  • Raspberry Pi
  • Arduino Mega
  • Arduino UNO

Features

It is capable of testing human body temperature, heartbeat, oxygen volume and blood pressure measurement. Besides, it can say his name, country name, name of the Father of the Nation and the name of the Prime Minister with the greetings. All this speaks in Bengali and English.
